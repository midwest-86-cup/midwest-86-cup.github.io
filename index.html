Time Attack Series


Midwest 86 Cup is an grassroots time attack series founded by track enthusiasts as a way to foster their love of performance driving and the 86 platform. The community began in 2013 in Souther California and has since grown to include 5 regions in SoCal, NorCal, NorthEast, Midwest, and Rocky Mountains.

How to participate

Come out to any of our events and turn in a 86 Cup competition form at the track (forms will be available). Please check for the organization hosting the track day for specific requirements to drive your car on track.


Don’t worry too much about ‘go fast’ mods, the car is already well built for the track. Brake pads and brake fluid are pretty much all the 86, FR-S, and BRZ need to be enjoyed lap after lap! If you need help choosing pads or have other questions, join our Facebook community.

Midwest 86 Cup Rules

Midwest 86 Cup will be following the same rules as SoCal 86 Cup.

Classing

Since different cars have different modifications, we use a point system which assigns points for certain modifications based on how much they improve the performance on an average track. Cars will be grouped into classes based on total points.

Use the class points calculator to easily class your car: SoCal 86 Cup 2018 Class Point Calculator.


2018 Rules

We will be running the exact rules used by SoCal 86 Cup. See the rules on their site here: SoCal 86 Cup - Rules
