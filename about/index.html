About 86 CUP

Midwest 86 Cup brings 86/FR-S/BRZ time attack to Illinois, Wisconsin, Indiana, and Michigan

86 Cup is a one chassis time attack series, typically run in a HPDE environment. We run alongside existing time attack and HPDE events. Midwest 86 Cup is a non-profit, community driven effort.

Utilizing a rule set that is specific to the platforms, the 86 Cup aims to provide a friendly, safe environment in which Toyota 86, Scion FR-S, and Subaru BRZ enthusiasts can hone and showcase their driving skills.

86 Cup welcomes enthusiasts of all skill levels. Whether you are a competitor or spectator, come hang out with us.

How to participate

Come out to any of our events and turn in a 86 Cup competition form at the track (forms will be available). Please check for the organization hosting the track day for specific requirements to drive your car on track.

Don’t worry too much about ‘go fast’ mods, the car is already well built for the track. Brake pads and brake fluid are pretty much all the 86, FR-S, and BRZ need to be enjoyed lap after lap! If you need help choosing pads or have other questions, join our Facebook community.
