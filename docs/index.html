Toyota 86 / Scion FR-S / Subaru BRZ
Time Attack Series

86 Cup got started out in California with a small group of 86/FR-S/BRZ owners and track enthusiasts as a way to foster their love of performance driving and the 86 platform. That community has grown and has spun off regional communities in SoCal, NorCal, NorthEast, and now the Midwest.

The 2018 season will be the first season of 86 Cup Time Attack in the Midwest region. This seriest is independently organized, community driven, and strives to provide beginnners and veterans with the perfect environment for developing and testing their driving skills with the 86 platform.

What do I need to participate?

  1. Toyota 86 / Scion FR-S / Subaru BRZ
  2. Competition brake pads and brake fluid - Not sure what to buy? Ask us here!
  3. Register for any of our events! - You can rent a tranponder from the organizers or run timing on your smartphone
